E-learning

A learning system based on formalized teaching but with the help of electronic resources is known as E-learning. Although teaching can take place in or out of the classroom, the use of computers and the Internet is a key component of E-learning. E-learning can also be referred to as the transfer of skills and knowledge through a network, and the delivery of education is done to a large number of recipients at the same time or at different times. Previously, this system was frowned upon because it was believed to lack the human element required for learning.
