About Us

Browse our course list to find something you want to learn. We have courses on a variety of topics and are adding more all the time.

TexnoCourse

TexnoCourse courses are divided into weeks. These contain different activities that you should aim to complete during the week, each built from a simple sequence of steps to help you learn. Each week is given a descriptive name, so you always know what's coming up, and you can even jump between them to see what's happening, or catch up early if you start late.

Complete Course

After completing the course, you will receive a digital certificate and then a printed certificate to help you demonstrate your learning if you have improved and met the requirements for the certificate. If you haven't upgraded, you still have a chance when you finish the course. If you upgrade, you'll be able to go back and complete any tests to earn your certification.
